PlatyTracker Map

About

PlatyTracker contributes to improved data quality of platypus abundance in the Great Sunshine Coast Region in order to;

-Increase understanding of habitat range to support conservation methods.

-Engage local residents to become custodians of their local freshwater systems.

-Advocate for both an upgrade in conservation status and the banning of opera house yabby traps in Queensland waters where platypus are present.

This program is mostly a self-funded initiative of Ecollaboration with some additional support from Sunshine Coast Council & Bendigo Bank Woombye

Welcome to the PlatyTracker Interactive Map

This map is desigened to highlight the ongoing PlatyTracker project by Ecollaboration, a volunteer project that is the first of it's kind in the Sunshine coast.

Each point highlights a survey conducted by a volunteer, navitgate to the side bar on the left to find useful links and tools to better understand this data.
